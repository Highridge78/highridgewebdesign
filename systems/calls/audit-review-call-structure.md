# Audit Review Call Structure

## 1) Align objective (2 min)
"Let’s align on your lead goals and priority fixes."

## 2) Confirm reality (6-8 min)
- Current lead flow
- Biggest bottleneck
- Response process

## 3) Walk priority issues (10 min)
- Issue -> impact -> recommended order

## 4) Implementation path (8 min)
- Phased path
- Full build path
- Tradeoffs

## 5) Close (3 min)
- Decide: scope next or hold with roadmap
