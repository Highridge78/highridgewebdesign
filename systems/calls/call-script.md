# Call Script (Audit Response to Opportunity)

## Goal
Turn audit interest into a qualified implementation opportunity.

## Call Length
25-35 minutes.

## Structure
1. Context + goal (2 min)
2. Their business goals + lead reality (8 min)
3. Audit review and priority discussion (10 min)
4. Fit + implementation path (8 min)
5. Next step decision (3-5 min)

## Opening
"Thanks for taking time. Goal today is to align on your lead goals, review the highest-impact website fixes, and decide whether implementation support is worth pursuing now."

## Discovery Questions
- Which lead types matter most to you right now?
- Where are leads currently coming from?
- What happens after someone submits or calls?
- Where do you think your website is underperforming most?
- How quickly do you want this improved?

## Transition to Solution
"Based on what you shared, the fastest path is [priority sequence]. If useful, I can scope this into a practical implementation plan."

## Close Options
- "Want me to send a scoped recommendation?"
- "Would you prefer phased implementation or full rebuild path?"

## If Not Ready
"No problem. I’ll send a priority roadmap so your team can still execute improvements in order."
