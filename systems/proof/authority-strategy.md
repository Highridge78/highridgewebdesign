# Authority Strategy (Without Lying)

## Principle

No fake proof. Demonstrate expertise through clear diagnostics and practical recommendations.

## Assets to Publish

1. Concept teardown videos (public)
2. Website critique clips (with respectful framing)
3. Before/after concept snippets (labeled concept work)
4. Educational posts: conversion and trust diagnostics
5. Anonymized audit insights (no fabricated results)

## Acceptable Language

- "Concept redesign"
- "Example audit"
- "Diagnostic walkthrough"
- "What I would change and why"

## Language to Avoid

- "Client results" (if none)
- "Case study" (if not real)
- "We helped X increase leads by Y%" (unless proven)
- "Trusted by" logos if untrue

## Positioning Line

"Highridge demonstrates capability through transparent website diagnostics and conversion-first recommendations."

## Content Cadence

- 2 teardown clips / week
- 1 written insight thread / week
- 1 anonymized audit breakdown / week

## CTA from Authority Content

"If you want this level of review for your own site, request a personalized Loom audit."
