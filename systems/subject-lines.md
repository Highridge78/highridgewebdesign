# Subject Line Bank

## Permission-first

- Quick website audit idea for [Business Name]
- Noticed 3 conversion leaks on your site
- Useful if I record a short website review?

## Direct-send Loom

- Recorded this audit for [Business Name]
- Quick personalized website audit
- 7-minute website review for your team

## Follow-ups

- Re: your website audit
- Want the priority fix list?
- Should I send the 1-page summary?
- Close the loop?

## Rules

- Keep plain and human.
- No clickbait.
- No fake urgency.
- Mention relevance (business name or site context).
