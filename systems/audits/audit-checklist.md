# Website Audit Checklist (Full)

Use this for a full Loom audit (10-15 minutes prep, 6-10 minute video).

## 1) Business + Offer Clarity
- [ ] Can I tell what they do in 5 seconds?
- [ ] Is the core service obvious above the fold?
- [ ] Is the service area visible?
- [ ] Is the offer specific (not generic "contact us")?
- [ ] Is there a clear next step for a ready buyer?

**If broken, why it matters:** confusion at first click lowers trust and kills lead intent.

## 2) First Impression + Credibility
- [ ] Does the homepage look current and trustworthy?
- [ ] Is branding cohesive (logo, typography, colors)?
- [ ] Any obvious low-trust signals (stock overload, clutter, poor spacing)?
- [ ] Are key trust proofs visible (licenses, years, reviews, guarantees)?

**If broken, why it matters:** first-impression distrust pushes leads to competitors.

## 3) CTA Strategy + Lead Paths
- [ ] Primary CTA visible without scrolling?
- [ ] CTA copy specific ("Book Estimate", "Call Now")?
- [ ] Is there a sticky/always-available CTA on mobile?
- [ ] Are CTA paths consistent across sections?
- [ ] Is phone action easy to tap on mobile?

**If broken, why it matters:** no clear action path = lost conversions.

## 4) Navigation + Information Architecture
- [ ] Main nav labels clear and useful?
- [ ] Services grouped logically?
- [ ] Contact/quote path one click away?
- [ ] Any dead-end or confusing pages?
- [ ] Footer supports navigation and trust?

**If broken, why it matters:** confused users drop instead of inquiring.

## 5) Service Page Conversion Strength
- [ ] Dedicated pages for key services?
- [ ] Service pages include outcomes, process, proof, CTA?
- [ ] Service area and job types mentioned?
- [ ] FAQs handling buying objections?

**If broken, why it matters:** weak service pages fail high-intent buyers.

## 6) Trust Signal Depth
- [ ] Real owner/team photos?
- [ ] Reviews/testimonial placement and context?
- [ ] Location credibility (address, service area, map cues)?
- [ ] Compliance/insurance/license badges where relevant?
- [ ] Guarantees/warranty/risk-reversal language?

**If broken, why it matters:** local buyers need safety before action.

## 7) Contact + Form Friction
- [ ] Form length reasonable?
- [ ] Required fields minimal?
- [ ] Is contact method choice offered (call/form/text)?
- [ ] Confirmation/status messaging clear?
- [ ] Is follow-up expectation stated?

**If broken, why it matters:** friction at intent moment costs leads.

## 8) Mobile Usability
- [ ] Hero text readable on mobile?
- [ ] Buttons large enough and not crowded?
- [ ] Menu easy to use one-handed?
- [ ] Layout stable (no jumping/overlap)?
- [ ] Tap targets accessible throughout?

**If broken, why it matters:** most local traffic is mobile; poor UX leaks demand.

## 9) Performance (Obvious)
- [ ] Hero loads quickly on 4G?
- [ ] Images appear optimized (no massive files)?
- [ ] Any jank from heavy animations?
- [ ] Core sections interactive quickly?

**If broken, why it matters:** speed affects bounce, trust, and calls.

## 10) SEO + Local Discoverability (Obvious)
- [ ] Proper title/meta intent on homepage?
- [ ] Service + city relevance in headings/content?
- [ ] Dedicated pages by service and/or location?
- [ ] Internal links between service pages?
- [ ] Contact NAP consistency signals?

**If broken, why it matters:** weak discoverability limits inbound pipeline.

## 11) Conversion Diagnosis
- [ ] Biggest trust leak identified?
- [ ] Biggest conversion leak identified?
- [ ] Fastest quick win identified?
- [ ] Strategic rebuild direction identified?

---

## Issue Categories
- Messaging / Clarity
- Trust / Credibility
- Conversion / CTA
- UX / Mobile
- Structure / IA
- Performance
- SEO / Local

## Severity Scoring
- **S1 Critical**: likely blocking leads now.
- **S2 High**: strong conversion drag.
- **S3 Medium**: meaningful improvement area.
- **S4 Low**: polish / secondary gain.

## Quick Wins Block (for each audit)
List 3 items doable quickly:
1. CTA clarity/action copy fix
2. Above-the-fold trust cue placement
3. Form friction reduction

## Strategic Direction Block
- Reposition headline + offer
- Rebuild page hierarchy around buyer intent
- Add trust architecture and local proof cues
- Strengthen service pages for qualified leads
