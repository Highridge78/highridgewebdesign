# KPI Dashboard (Simple)

Track weekly:

- Prospects added
- Rapid screens completed
- Looms recorded
- Looms sent
- Reply rate
- Positive reply rate
- Calls booked
- Proposal opportunities
- Win rate (when applicable)

## Minimum Viable Targets (Solo)

- 25 prospects screened / week
- 10 Looms recorded / week
- 10 Looms sent / week
- 20+ follow-up touches / week
- 2-4 calls booked / week target range (early stage)
