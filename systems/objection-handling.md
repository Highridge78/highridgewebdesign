# Objection Handling Bank

## "We already have someone"

"Totally fair. If they’re handling execution, this can still act as a priority blueprint so your team fixes the highest-impact conversion issues first."

## "We’re not ready"

"Understood. Would it help if I send a staged roadmap: what to fix now, next, and later? That way you can move when timing is better."

## "What does this cost?"

"Depends on scope and speed. I can give a realistic range after we align on priorities and whether you want phased or full implementation."

## "Can you just send pricing?"

"I can send ranges, but they won’t be useful without matching scope to your goals. Better to align on the top priorities first so pricing is accurate."

## "We only need small tweaks"

"Sometimes small fixes are enough. If the structural conversion issues are larger, I’ll tell you that directly so you can decide based on ROI, not preference."

## "We’ll think about it"

"Makes sense. Want me to send a short decision summary with the top 3 impacts so the team can evaluate quickly?"
