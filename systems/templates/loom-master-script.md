# Loom Master Script (6-9 Minutes)

## 1) Hook (20 sec)
"I reviewed your website and found a few clear opportunities to improve trust and qualified lead flow."

## 2) Scope (20 sec)
"I checked homepage clarity, CTA path, trust signals, mobile usability, and lead friction."

## 3) Top Issues (4-6 min)
For each issue:
- What I see
- Why it matters
- What to change first

## 4) Quick Wins (60 sec)
"If you only fix three things first: [1], [2], [3]."

## 5) Strategic Direction + Close (45 sec)
"If useful, next step is aligning these fixes into an implementation plan based on your goals and timeline."
