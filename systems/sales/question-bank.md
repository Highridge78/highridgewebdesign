# Discovery Question Bank

## Business + Pipeline

- What type of leads are most valuable to you?
- What percentage of leads come from website calls/forms?
- Which services need more demand right now?

## Conversion Friction

- Where do you feel prospects drop off?
- What objections do leads raise most often?
- How quickly does your team respond currently?

## Decision + Timing

- Who decides on website/growth initiatives?
- What timeline are you targeting?
- Do you prefer phased execution or full rebuild?
