# Offer Definition - Free Website Audit for Local Businesses

## One-Sentence Offer
A free, personalized Loom website audit that identifies the top conversion and trust issues likely costing your business qualified leads.

## Short Paragraph Offer
Highridge records a personalized website audit for local service businesses that want better lead flow. You receive a short Loom walkthrough plus a written priority summary showing what is hurting trust, clarity, and conversion, what to fix first, and what strategic direction to take next.

## Who It Is For
- Local service businesses that rely on inbound calls/forms
- Contractors and home service companies
- Businesses with active lead goals and visible website gaps
- Owners/managers willing to improve conversion performance

## Who It Is NOT For
- Businesses seeking the cheapest possible website
- Businesses with no urgency to improve lead flow
- Projects needing only cosmetic tweaks with no growth intent

## Deliverables
- 1 personalized Loom audit (typically 6-9 minutes)
- 1 written audit summary (priority actions + strategic direction)
- Optional call invitation to discuss implementation path

## Format + Timing
- Loom video link + written summary document
- Delivery target: within 48-72 hours after prospect selection

## Why It Is Valuable
- Shows exactly where the site is leaking trust/leads
- Gives practical priority order, not vague recommendations
- Helps business decide what to fix first without guessing

## What Happens After Delivery
- If interested: short strategy call
- If fit is strong: scoped implementation recommendation
- If not ready: keep written priorities for future use

## Outreach Description Snippet
"I recorded a short personalized website audit showing the top issues likely hurting lead conversion and what I would fix first."

## On-Site Description Snippet
"Get a free personalized website audit: a short Loom walkthrough + written action plan focused on trust, conversion, and qualified lead flow."
