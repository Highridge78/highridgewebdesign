# Reply Handling SOP

## SLA

Respond within 4 business hours.

## Positive Reply

1. Thank them
2. Offer 2 time slots
3. State call purpose and duration
4. Log status: Replied -> Call Booking

## Neutral Reply

1. Clarify what they want (summary, call, scope)
2. Offer low-friction next step
3. Set follow-up date

## Objection Reply

1. Acknowledge
2. Reframe around priority and risk
3. Offer small next step (priority list/call)

## No-Show / Delay

- Send respectful reschedule note same day
- Offer two new slots
- If no reply, return to follow-up cadence
