# Ideal Targets Framework

## Best First Niches
1. HVAC
2. Plumbing
3. Electrical
4. Roofing
5. Remodeling / General Contractors

## Why These Niches
- Depend on inbound leads
- Trust-sensitive buying decision
- High value per job
- Frequent mobile search intent
- Clear downside of weak websites

## Strong Fit Signals
- Website clearly outdated or unclear
- Active local service area
- Paid ads or visible growth intent
- Service pages are thin/weak
- Owner or manager contact identifiable

## Weak Fit Signals
- Excellent modern conversion site already
- Non-lead-dependent business model
- No service area focus
- No digital urgency

## Red Flags
- Price-only posture in all messaging
- No decision-maker access
- No urgency + no timeline

## Budget Likelihood Indicators
- Multiple trucks/team members
- Active hiring
- Paid media usage
- Premium service offerings

## Website Weakness Indicators
- Generic hero headline
- No trust architecture
- Vague CTA
- Poor mobile readability
- Thin service pages

## Niche Scoring Model (1-10)
- Lead dependency (0-3)
- Trust sensitivity (0-3)
- Job value (0-2)
- Site improvement potential (0-2)

## Business Scoring Model (1-10)
- Website weakness severity (0-4)
- Buyer accessibility (0-2)
- Budget likelihood (0-2)
- Urgency signals (0-2)

## Quick Qualification Checklist
- [ ] Obvious conversion leaks
- [ ] Lead-dependent business
- [ ] Commercially viable target
- [ ] Contact path available
- [ ] Good before/after potential
