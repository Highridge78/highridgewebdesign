# Sales Workflow (Audit to Opportunity)

## Funnel Stages

1. Prospect identified
2. Rapid screen completed
3. Loom recorded
4. Loom sent + summary sent
5. Reply received
6. Call booked
7. Discovery + audit call completed
8. Proposal opportunity
9. Won / Lost / Dormant

## Stage Exit Rules

- Do not send Loom without clear top 3 issues.
- Do not book call without confirming business need.
- Do not propose without fit + urgency + buyer involvement.

## Qualification Signals on Reply

- "Can we fix this quickly?"
- "How much would this cost?"
- "Can you walk my team through this?"
- "What would you prioritize first?"

## Disqualification Signals

- Price-only shopping with no outcomes discussion
- No urgency, no owner involvement, no clear decision path
- Misaligned scope (e.g., pure social-only ask)
