# Prospecting SOP (Fast, Daily)

## Goal

Find high-opportunity local businesses and decide quickly whether they deserve a Loom.

## Daily Inputs

- Google Maps category searches
- Local directories / chamber listings
- Facebook business pages
- Local ads (search + social)

## Timebox

- 60-90 minutes total prospecting per day
- 5 minutes max per prospect in screening stage

## Workflow

1. Build a list of 20 businesses in one niche + one geography cluster.
2. Rapid-screen each site with `rapid-audit-checklist.md`.
3. Score with quick model (fit + weakness + lead dependency).
4. Select top 3-5 for Loom recording.
5. Log all details in tracker immediately.

## What to Capture

- business name, website, city, niche
- contact person (if available)
- key issue hypothesis (1 line)
- priority (A/B/C)
- next action + date

## Avoid Time Waste

- Skip businesses with strong modern sites + clear conversion paths.
- Skip no-contact/no-owner visibility unless niche is excellent.
- Skip low-commercial-intent categories.

## Prospect Note Template

- Snapshot: [business + niche + city]
- Why fit: [lead-dependent signal]
- Top leaks: [3 bullets]
- Priority: [A/B/C]
- Loom angle: [core theme]
