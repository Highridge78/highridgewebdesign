# Loom Script (Master)

## Target Length
- 6 to 9 minutes.
- 4 to 6 issues max.

## Structure
1. Hook (20-30 sec)
2. What I reviewed (20 sec)
3. Highest-impact issues (4-6 min)
4. Quick wins (60-90 sec)
5. Strategic direction + close (45-60 sec)

## Opening
"Hey [Name], I recorded this because I noticed a few fixable website issues that are likely costing [business name] qualified leads. I want to walk you through the highest-impact ones quickly."

## Context
"I reviewed your homepage, service flow, mobile experience, and lead path. I focused on what affects trust, clarity, and conversion."

## Issue Loop (repeat)
- "What I see"
- "Why it matters"
- "What to change"

Example:
"First, your hero headline is too broad. A new visitor cannot instantly tell the exact service outcome. That creates hesitation and lower inquiry rates. I would tighten this to [specific promise + service + location], then place one clear CTA right beside it."

## Transition Lines
- "Second issue and this one is a conversion leak..."
- "Related to that, your mobile path adds friction..."
- "The good news is this is straightforward to improve..."

## Quick Wins Segment
"If you only changed three things first, I would do: [1], [2], [3]. Those alone should improve clarity and lead flow."

## Close
"If useful, I can map this into a practical execution plan for your business model and service area. Either way, I hope this gives you clear next steps."

## Guardrails
- Be specific, not theatrical.
- Never claim guaranteed numeric outcomes.
- Do not over-deliver implementation details line-by-line.
- Keep authority calm and direct.
