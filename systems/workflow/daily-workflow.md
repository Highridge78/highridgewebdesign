# Daily Workflow SOP (Solo Operator)

## Daily Block Plan (3-4 hours focused)
1. Prospecting + screening (60-90 min)
2. Loom prep + recording (90-120 min)
3. Outreach + follow-ups (45-60 min)
4. CRM update + next actions (15 min)

## Minimum Viable Daily Output
- 5-8 rapid screens
- 2 Loom recordings
- 2 Loom sends
- 4-8 follow-up messages

## Stop Rules
- Stop researching once you have today's Loom queue.
- Stop polishing Loom after one re-record unless factual error.
- Do not exceed 10 minutes prep per audit.
