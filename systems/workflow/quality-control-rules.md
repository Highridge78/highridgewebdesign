# Quality Control Rules

1. No Loom sent without top 3 issues + why-it-matters logic.
2. No outreach without business-specific first sentence.
3. No call booked without confirming business objective.
4. No proposal opportunity without fit + urgency + buyer access.
5. Every lead row must have next action/date.

## Loom Quality Bar
- Clear audio
- Under 9 minutes unless requested
- 4-6 issues max
- Commercial language, no jargon dumping
