# Outreach Sequence (Loom Audit Engine)

## Sequence Overview
- Day 0: Permission-first or direct-send Loom
- Day 2: Follow-up #1 (no reply)
- Day 5: Follow-up #2 (value recap)
- Day 9: Follow-up #3 (close loop)
- Day 14+: Dormant/recycle later

## Template 1: Permission-First Email
Subject: Quick website audit idea for [Business Name]

Hi [Name],

I help local service businesses improve websites that are underperforming as lead assets.

I spotted a few fixable conversion issues on [business website].
If useful, I can record a short personalized Loom audit (no charge) showing exactly what I would change first.

Want me to send it?

- [Your Name]
Highridge Web Design

## Template 2: Direct-Send Loom Email
Subject: Recorded this quick audit for [Business Name]

Hi [Name],

I reviewed [business website] and recorded a short personalized audit:
[Loom Link]

I focused on practical fixes tied to lead conversion (clarity, trust, CTA flow, and mobile friction).

If helpful, I can also send a written summary with priority actions.

- [Your Name]
Highridge Web Design

## Template 3: Follow-up (No Response)
Subject: Re: quick audit for [Business Name]

Hi [Name],

Bumping this in case it got buried.
The audit points are practical and focused on lead flow, not cosmetic redesign advice.

If you want, I can send a 1-page written summary instead of video.

- [Your Name]

## Template 4: Follow-up (Viewed, No Reply)
Subject: Want the priority action list?

Hi [Name],

Saw you viewed the audit. If useful, I can send a short priority list:
1) what to fix first
2) why it matters
3) what can wait

Should I send that over?

- [Your Name]

## Template 5: Positive Reply Response
Subject: Re: next step

Perfect. Happy to help.

Here are two options:
- [Option A Date/Time]
- [Option B Date/Time]

On the call, we’ll keep it practical: biggest leaks, fix priority, and whether implementation support makes sense.

- [Your Name]

## Template 6: Last Touch / Close Loop
Subject: Close the loop?

Hi [Name],

I’ll close this thread for now.
If you want the written audit summary or a quick strategy call later, reply with "audit" and I’ll reopen it.

- [Your Name]

## Short DM Templates

### Follow-up DM (No Reply)
\"No pressure - if useful, I can send a 1-page written summary instead of video.\"

## Outreach Hooks (Non-Spam)
- \"I noticed a few fixable conversion leaks on your site.\"
- \"I recorded a short walkthrough focused on trust + lead flow.\"
- \"I kept this practical: what to fix first and why.\"

## Soft CTA Language
- \"Want me to send it?\"
- \"Useful if I share the short version?\"
- \"Should I send the priority list?\"
- \"Happy to walk you through it if you want.\"

## Calendar Invitation Language
\"If easier, we can do a short 25-minute call to prioritize fixes. I can do [Option A] or [Option B].\"
